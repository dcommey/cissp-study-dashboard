# CISSP Practice Quiz - Video 2: Security 101

Test your knowledge on the core security concepts covered in Video 2. Think from a governance and risk management perspective.

### Question 1
An organization recently suffered a data breach due to a zero-day exploit in its web server software. The security team had previously performed risk assessments, documented their architectures, applied all vendor-released patches up to the day of the attack, and continuously monitored network traffic. From a legal liability perspective, which of the following is correct?

A. The organization failed to exercise due care by not preventing the breach.  
B. The organization can demonstrate due care and due diligence by showing they researched risks and implemented standard protections.  
C. The security analyst who configured the firewall will be held personally liable for the data breach.  
D. The organization is compliant because zero-day exploits are explicitly exempt from all regulations.
