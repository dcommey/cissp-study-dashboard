# CISSP Practice Quiz - Video 3: Risk Management

Test your understanding of risk management concepts and calculations. Remember to apply the managerial mindset!

---

### Question 1
A security manager is assessing the risk of power outages to an enterprise database server valued at $100,000. History shows that a power outage occurs approximately once every two years, and each outage destroys about 30% of the database data, requiring expensive recovery. What is the Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE) for this threat?

A. $30,000  
B. $15,000  
C. $50,000  
D. $200,000

### Question 2
An organization wants to conduct a qualitative risk assessment regarding a new cloud migration project. The project lead wants to collect honest opinions from security engineers, project managers, and financial executives without causing conflict or letting senior directors dominate the discussion. Which assessment method is best suited for this goal?

A. Brainstorming workshops  
B. One-on-one structured interviews  
C. The Delphi technique  
D. Quantitative financial analysis

### Question 3
A company decides to host its email servers with a third-party SaaS provider to avoid managing the associated hardware and software vulnerabilities. They also purchase a cyber insurance policy to cover potential breach costs. How should these risk response strategies be categorized?

A. Risk Mitigation and Risk Acceptance  
B. Risk Transference and Risk Transference  
C. Risk Avoidance and Risk Transference  
D. Risk Avoidance and Risk Mitigation

### Question 4

B. Preventative Technical Control  
C. Corrective Administrative Control
